Description

The Kit is a customisable light based sensor prop, with four sensors, a control board, power supply and output device a more detialed description will be posted in future, for now please watch a small informal demo video i made. If the embedded player is to slow to buffer below feel free to click on the following youtube link. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IYQvVgOtml0

Quick note: The control board lights are for the owner to help with configuring the sensitivity adjustment via the potentiometer not generally for the user to experience. To make it more of a challenge a usefull prop idea for example would be to have four opaque jars in a row. Pulling off the correct jar lids in the correct pattern implmented by the owner would be the puzzle and unlock the next clue or reward for the user.
